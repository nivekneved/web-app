# Travel Lounge Features - Web App

## Public Services

- **Cruises**: Full listing of available sea voyages with dynamic filtering.
- **Flights**: International and regional flight booking options.
- **Hotels**:
  - **Mauritius**: Premium resort listings with dynamic room pricing.
  - **Rodrigues**: Dedicated island experience with specific guest houses and hotels.
- **Day Packages**: "Sea" and "Land" activity filtering for local residents and tourists.
- **Group Tours**: Region-based tours (Asia, Europe, Middle East) with detailed itineraries.

## User Experience

- **Modern Navbar**:
  - Simplified "Hotels" direct link.
  - Dropdown "About" menu with "Inside Travel Lounge" and "Our Team".
  - Sticky glassmorphism header.
- **About & Team**: High-impact storytelling and professional staff profiles.
- **Wishlist**: Save favorite services for future bookings.
- **Responsive Design**: Mobile-first approach for all listing and detail pages.
